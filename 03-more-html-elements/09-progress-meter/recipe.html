Chockolate Chip Cooking Recipe!

Ingrediants

  • 2 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 cup unsalted butter
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 cup packed light-brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 teaspoons pure vanilla extract
  • 2 large eggs

Baking Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a small bowl, whisk together the flour and baking soda; set aside.
  3. In a large bowl, beat the butter and sugars with an electric mixer on medium speed until light and fluffy. Add the salt, vanilla, and eggs, and beat until well mixed, about 1 minute.
  4. Gradually add the flour mixture, mixing until just combined. Stir in the chocolate chips.
  5. Drop heaping tablespoon-sized balls of dough about 2 inches apart on baking sheets lined with parchment paper.
  6. Bake until cookies are golden around the edges but still soft in the center, 8 to 10 minutes. Remove from oven, and let cool on baking sheet 1 to 2 minutes. Transfer to a wire rack, and let cool completely.
